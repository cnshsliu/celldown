Welcome to Celldown.

Celldown is a desktop application for mind mapping and personal knowledge management. Celldown supports synchronized Markdown editing and previewing. A mind mapping node is also treated as a wiki word, which is clickable if it appears in another node's content. Every piece of your mind is cross-linked this way to form your mind universe.

Celldown keep evolving to make itself one of the most feature rich and effective mind management tool for you. Download it now to explore!
